Inequality Of Wealth In America

Stirling Waite (stirlingw@gmail.com), Jee Loong Chew (jee.loong@gmail.com), Kirill B (kirill00k@gmail.com)

Our perception of the distribution of wealth in America is not quite accurate, especially in terms of inequalities and the actual real numbers. Are only the top 10% earners in the economy living “life of luxury”, and the other 90% getting the scraps? How much money does it take to “feel rich” in America?
